This is my professional story.

Cigar Club & the Gatsby

The first part of my career was spent owning and operating a cigar store and bar called Cigar Club and later a bar and live music venue called the Gatsby.

I took over Cigar Club from my mom Kandis who started it and ran it successfully for 8 years. We worked hard and grew the business and had a really successful 4 year run after that, building a loyal customer base and a live music program that garnered local recognition.

We had the opportunity to evolve and from the ashes of Cigar Club rose the Gatsby. I developed an entirely new concept and opened the Gatsby as a live music venue with an innovative bar program.

Nordstrom

I spent the next seven years of my career working for the best fashion retailer in the world, Nordstrom.

I went from Austin to Dallas to Nashville (where I met Lauren, the love of my life) and finally to Miami. I started out selling jeans and t-shirts and finished up leading the largest designer shoe floor in the country, stopping in every men's department along the way.

I got to meet so many inspiring people, lead some truly amazing teams and learned countless valuable lessons about leadership.

And my wardrobe got A LOT better.
